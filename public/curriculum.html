What's Included in the Classic Package

Instruction20 hours of instruction in traditional Calabrian cooking: pasta, breads, pork, fish, cheese, vegetables. 4 hours per day x 5 days.
LunchLunch after each class, including wine.
Field TripsVisits to local farms, vineyards, markets, and specialty producers.
SupportTranslator/Certified Guide, Driver and Van (when applicable).
TransfersTransfer from Paola Train Station or Lamezia Airport.
Not IncludedAirfare, Transfers from Lamazia Airport, Dinners (with few exceptions).

Location

Marano MarchesatoLocated in the Province of Cosenza, Region of Calabria, Italy.

Monday - Traditional Calabrian Appetizers

Join us every Monday for a culinary journey through the flavors of Calabria with our “Traditional Calabrian Appetizers” class. Immerse yourself in the rich culture and taste of Southern Italy as you learn to create authentic appetizers that are a staple in Calabrian cuisine.

Tuesday - Calabrian Pasta (Primi) Class

Join us every Tuesday for a culinary journey through the flavors of Calabria with our “Calabrian Pasta (Primi)” class. Immerse yourself in the rich culture and taste of Southern Italy as you learn to create authentic pasta dishes that are the main course in Calabrian cuisine.

Wednesday - Calabrian Meat Dishes (Secondo) Class

Join us every Wednesday for a culinary journey through the flavors of Calabria with our “Calabrian Meat Dishes (Secondo)” class. Immerse yourself in the rich culture and taste of Southern Italy as you learn to create the meat dishes that are a staple in Calabrian cuisine.
