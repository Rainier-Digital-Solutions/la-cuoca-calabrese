Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to the class?

Just bring yourself and a willingness to learn. All ingredients and tools are provided.

Are the classes suitable for beginners?

Yes, our classes are designed for all skill levels, from beginners to advanced cooks.

Can I book a private class?

Yes, we offer private classes for individuals, couples, and groups. Contact us for more details.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, please let us know in advance, and we will do our best to accommodate your dietary needs.

How can I book a class or tour?

You can book a class or tour through our website or by contacting us directly.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel up to 48 hours before the class for a full refund. After that, no refunds are available.
